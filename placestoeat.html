Tupelo Honey Cafe

Discover Southern Soul
If you're headed to Asheville, North Carolina—the heart of the Blue Ridge Mountains—you owe it to yourself to stop at Tupelo Honey Cafe, a restaurant that captures the essence of Southern hospitality and flavor like nowhere else.Every dish is crafted with locally sourced ingredients and a deep respect for tradition. Whether you're a die-hard foodie or just someone looking for a satisfying, soul-nourishing meal, Tupelo Honey delivers.So whether you're a local or just passing through, do yourself a favor: Make a reservation at Tupelo Honey Cafe. One bite, and you'll understand why it's one of Asheville's most beloved culinary gems.

Daddy Mac's Down Home Dive

Down-Home Flavor, Dive-Bar Soul
Looking for a place where the food is bold, the drinks are strong, and the atmosphere feels like your best friend's backyard party? Daddy Mac's Down Home Dive in Asheville, NC, is calling your name.This isn't just another Southern joint—it's a full-blown experience that combines comfort food with creative flair, served in a space that's equal parts dive bar and soul kitchen. Think neon signs, cold beer, and the kind of energy that makes you want to stay a while.
Let's talk food: the menu is packed with Southern staples taken to the next level—like their juicy smoked wings, award-worthy mac 'n'cheese, and piled-high BBQ sandwiches that are unapologetically messy and mouthwatering. It's the kind of food that sticks to your ribs and your memory.
